Rune was created by Olivia Johnson, a senior at the Maryland Institute College of Art. She is interested in experimenting with digital tools, social design, and discovering new podcasts.

Currently, Olivia is planning her upcoming degree project. After creating Rune, she expects to incorporate an interactive aspect into the project. She will not, however, try to create an exhibition that requires printing, trimming, and hanging new content every day.

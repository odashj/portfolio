Hi, my name is Olivia Johnson.

I craft socially thoughtful work through design and data.

For the past three years, I’ve helped lead the design process at Sosolimited, where I’ve developed interactive platforms, demystified complex data, and streamlined user experiences.

As a student and in my own time, I’ve sought out projects that empower communities and create positive social impact, including a magazine funded by the Baltimore City Health Department to address the HIV crisis in Baltimore, a website and campaign that challenges preconceived notions about Socialism, and a reimagining of the United States Flags using census data to generate graphics.

Want to know more about me or get in touch? My resumé can be viewed here and I can be reached at olivia@o-j.co.

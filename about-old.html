Olivia Johnson combines data, interactivity, and graphic design to produce work that aims to make a positive social impact on society.

She currently works as a designer at Sosolimited, a studio that creates sensory spaces driven by design and technology in Boston, MA.

Olivia's work has been featured in Brand New, Core77 and PRINT. She has exhibited in MICA GD's Alumni Invitational Exhibition, Greater Good: Social Design Invitational, AIGA Boston's New Voices Unique Visions, and Shenzhen International Design Festival, and has been a featured speaker for Design Madison.

Please contact olivia@o-j.co for questions and inquiries. Resumé available upon request.
